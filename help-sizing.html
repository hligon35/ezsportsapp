Sizing & Fit

EZ Sports Sizing Guide

Choosing the right size is key for safety, performance, and satisfaction. Use the tips below to find the best fit for netting, gear, and apparel.

Netting Sizing

  • Custom Netting: Measure the width, height, and depth of your space. Add 6-12 inches to each dimension for proper fit and tensioning.
  • Batting Cages: Standard sizes are 12'W x 12'H x 55'L, but we can custom-build to your specs. Contact us for help with unique layouts.
  • Replacement Panels: Measure the area to be covered and match the mesh size and twine gauge to your existing net.

Gear Sizing

  • Bats: Use our bat sizing chart or consult your coach. Most youth players use 27-31"; adults 32-34".
  • Gloves: Measure from the tip of your index finger to the start of your wrist. Infielders: 11-11.75"; Outfielders: 12-13".
  • Batting Gloves: Measure hand circumference just below the knuckles. See product page for size chart.

Apparel Sizing

  • Refer to the size chart on each product page. Most apparel runs true to size unless noted.
  • If between sizes, size up for a looser fit or down for a tighter fit.

Still unsure? Contact support for personalized sizing help or custom netting advice.
